7-Stage Purification Process

Every drop of Zuvia packaged drinking water undergoes a rigorous 7-stage purification process to ensure purity, safety, and balanced mineral content in every sip.

1. Source Water

Collected from protected underground water sources.

2. Sand Filtration

Removes large particles and suspended impurities.

3. Carbon Filtration

Eliminates chlorine, odor, and organic contaminants.

4. Reverse Osmosis (RO)

Removes dissolved salts, heavy metals, and harmful substances.

5. UV Purification

Destroys bacteria, viruses, and harmful microorganisms.

6. Ozonation

Provides additional disinfection and freshness.

7. Mineralization

Adds essential minerals to enhance taste and health benefits.
